Privacy Policy

How Dolphin collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: March 2026
1

Introduction

At Dolphin, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platform. By accessing or using Dolphin, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users. The types of information we collect include:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile details provided during registration.
  • User Content: Profile images and other content you choose to upload to the platform.
  • Communications: Messages and interactions between users within the platform.
  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and analytics data.
3

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Creating and managing your user account.

  • Preventing fraud and maintaining platform security.

4

Sharing of Information

Dolphin does not sell your personal data. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • To protect the rights, privacy, safety, or property of Dolphin and our users.
5

Data Storage and Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6

Cookies and Tracking

Dolphin uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our service and hold certain information. Cookies are files with small amounts of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier.

7

User Rights

You have certain rights regarding your information:

  • You can request the deletion of your account and associated data.
  • You can contact us regarding any privacy concerns.
8

We may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our service. These third parties have access to your Personal Information only for specific tasks (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics) and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

9

Our service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

10
